The following criterion is used to determine if a cell should be refined:

\[|\nabla T|\,|\mathbf{r}| > h\]

where

\[\mathbf{r} = \sqrt{\Delta x_1^2 + \Delta x_2^2 + \Delta x_3^2}\]

and \(h\) is stored in refinement_threshold
